Our Mission

To empower aspiring entrepreneurs in transforming innovative ideas into sustainable, successful ventures by providing the resources, mentorship, and practical experience needed to build viable companies.

Our Vision

Empowering Student Entrepreneurs to turn their innovative ideas into successful, lasting businesses, and becoming a hub of innovation in engineering.

Principles

  • Innovation: Creative thinking and original solutions.
  • Collaboration: Valuing teamwork and partnerships.
  • Integrity: Upholding ethical standards.
  • Sustainability: Responsible entrepreneurship.
  • Empowerment: Supporting entrepreneurial risks.
  • Adaptability: Flexibility in the face of challenges.
  • Impact: Creating meaningful value for society.
  • Continuous Learning: Ongoing education and growth.

Objectives

Our primary objective is to streamline the startup process and foster a collaborative community. We aim to enhance visibility and presence through pitch competitions and startup showcases, while providing continuous support to members.
